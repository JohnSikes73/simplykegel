Frequently asked

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → your name at the top → Subscriptions → Simply Kegel → Cancel Subscription. Cancelling before your 7-day free trial ends means you won't be charged.

Does the app work offline?
Yes. Simply Kegel runs entirely on your device. An internet connection is only required the first time you start your free trial or restore a purchase.

Where is my progress data stored?
Locally on your iPhone, in the app's sandboxed storage. Nothing is uploaded to any server. If you delete the app, your progress history is deleted with it.

Is this app a medical device?
No. Simply Kegel is a general wellness and fitness app. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. If you have pelvic pain, incontinence, erectile dysfunction, are pregnant or postpartum, or are recovering from surgery, please consult a healthcare professional or pelvic-floor physiotherapist before starting any pelvic floor program.
