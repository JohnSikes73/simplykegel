Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 April 2026 · Last updated: 23 April 2026

Summary

Simply Kegel is a privacy-first iOS app. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you. We don't use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party trackers. We don't have a server.

This policy explains what little data the app does touch and how it's handled.

Who we are

Simply Kegel ("the app", "we", "us") is developed by Prantik Biswas, an individual developer based in India. You can reach us at simplykegel@gmail.com.

What information the app stores

The app saves the following data in its local sandboxed storage on your iPhone (iOS UserDefaults):

  • Your selected exercise routine and its settings (reps, hold durations, rest times).
  • Your progress history — seconds of pull and push exercises completed each day.
  • Your preferences — selected level, theme (Light/Dark), reminder times.
  • A record of whether your 7-day free trial has started.

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to iCloud unless iOS itself backs it up as part of an encrypted device backup that you control.

What we do not collect

  • We do not ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, or any account credentials. The app has no login.
  • We do not collect your location, contacts, photos, microphone audio, camera video, health data, or motion data.
  • We do not use any analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Sentry, no Crashlytics — nothing).
  • We do not use advertising SDKs and we do not show ads.
  • We do not share or sell any data, because we do not have any data.

Subscriptions and payments

Simply Kegel offers a subscription that starts with a 7-day free trial. Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple's App Store using Apple's StoreKit framework. We never see your payment card, Apple ID, billing address, or any personally identifying purchase information.

Apple provides us with a non-identifying receipt that tells the app whether your subscription is active. That receipt is used locally by the app to unlock paid features and is not transmitted anywhere by us.

Apple's own privacy policy governs how Apple handles your payment and Apple ID data: apple.com/legal/privacy.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device using iOS's built-in notification system. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely by iOS on your phone. They are not sent through any server and we never learn when (or whether) you received them.

Permissions the app requests

  • Notifications — only if you enable daily reminders. You can revoke this at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Simply Kegel.

The app does not request any other iOS permissions (no camera, photos, microphone, location, health, contacts, or motion access).

Children's privacy

Simply Kegel is rated 17+ on the App Store and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone — including children — because we do not collect data at all.

Third-party services

The only third party the app interacts with is Apple, for the purpose of processing subscriptions through the App Store. No other third-party services, SDKs, or networks are integrated.

Data retention and deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you delete your data by deleting the app. Uninstalling Simply Kegel removes every setting, routine, and statistic the app has ever saved on your device.

Inside the app, you can also clear your progress history at any time via Settings.

Security

App data is stored in iOS's standard app sandbox, which is protected by iOS's built-in data protection (encrypted at rest when your device is locked with a passcode or biometric).

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will change. Material changes will also be announced in the app's update notes on the App Store.
